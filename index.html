Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

When should I see a cardiologist? +
If you have chest pain, shortness of breath, dizzy spells or irregular heartbeats — or risk factors like high BP, diabetes, high cholesterol or family history.
What symptoms should never be ignored? +
Sudden or severe chest pain, pain radiating to the arm/jaw, extreme breathlessness, unexplained fainting or sudden palpitations — seek help immediately.
What is angioplasty? +
A minimally invasive procedure to open clogged heart arteries using a balloon, often with a stent placed to keep the artery open.
